Biography

I am a PhD candidate in Computer Science at Missouri University of Science and Technology, working under the supervision of Dr. Shubham Chatterjee. My research focuses on Large Language Models, Information Retrieval, and Data Science. My work bridges AI/NLP and information systems to address real-world problems.

Prior to joining Missouri S&T, I completed my Bachelor's degree in Applied Mathematics and Computer Science at Moscow Institute of Physics and Technology, where I worked on HPC/MPI applications. My academic journey has been driven by my passion for artificial intelligence and high-performance computing.
